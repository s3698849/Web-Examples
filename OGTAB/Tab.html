Follow SOPs and Stop Spreading Covid-19 - Stay Home Stay Safe

Wear Mask
Wash Hands
Keep Social Distance
Wear mask when you go outside and specially in rush place. Mask is very important gear because it can prevent you 40% from virus. if you have N95 mask well and good but if don't then wear simple surgical mask or mask made with cotton cloth or any other. Don't use surgical or medical mask more then 2 times.
Wash your hands regularly at home and when you enter in your house from outside. Use hand sanitizer if have otherwise use regular soap and wash your hands at least 20 seconds. Wash your hands before and after eat.
Maintain the distance of at least 3ft-6ft from other person specially when you at public place or in any market side. Social distance prevent you from virus and it also in th benefit of other peoples too. Stay at home and Save lifes.
